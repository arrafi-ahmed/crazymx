ClickEvent Priority

ClickEvent Priority is an option alongside the standard list. It allows users to purchase an access priority online for a small amount. With this priority, users can access the event quickly using a dedicated fast‑track line, while the entry ticket payment will be made at the counter.

Users who do not choose the Priority option can still enter via the standard list. The QR code for those who purchased priority will be identified as PAYMENT AT ENTRY with PRIORITY at the time of scanning.

Any tax aspects related to the purchase of priority are handled entirely by the organizer and the hosting venue.
